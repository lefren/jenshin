    • About Game

    Jenshin Impekz is an action-packed role-playing video game set in the fictional world of Teyvat. Players assume the role of the Traveler, who is searching for their lost sibling, exploring Teyvat, fighting enemies in real-time combat, completing quests, and acquiring new characters and weapons. With a diverse landscape, NPCs with unique routines, and a captivating story, Jenshin Impekz offers an immersive gaming experience.
